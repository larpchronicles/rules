# Monstrous Ability Rituals

* Ranks Correspond to the following Ritual Rank
    * Basic: Rank I
        * Active: 2+3
        * Passive: 5
    * Expert:  Rank III
        * Active 3+5
        * Passive 10
    * Advanced: Rank V
        * Active 5+10
        * Passive 15
    * High: Rank VII
        * Active 10+10
        * Passive 20
    * Master: Rank IX
        * Active 20+10
        * Passive 30
    * Grand: Rank X
        * Active 20+20
        * Passive 40
