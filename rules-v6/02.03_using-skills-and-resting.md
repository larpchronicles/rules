## Using Skills, Abilities, and Spells
A character's skills, abilities, and spells are the mechanical tools used to interact with the game world. Skills are generally always available, and are acquired using Skill Points. Abilities and spells cost resources (Stamina Points, Agility Points, or Mana Points, depending on the ability) to utilize. Such resources can be recovered by Resting.

### Battleboards: Tracking your skills
When you check in, you'll receive a **battleboard** along with your character sheet. Your battleboard is a tracking sheet that lists your character's abilities, spells, and resource pools. As you use abilities and spend resources during the event, you'll mark them off on the battleboard. This is how both you and staff keep track of what you've used and what you have remaining.

### Resting
Resting is how your character recovers spent resources and refreshes their abilities. After a Rest, your Mana Points, Stamina Points, and Agility Points return to full, and any per-Rest abilities are renewed.

To Rest, your character must be in the **In Town area** (the tavern and PC cabins). Head to Logistics, have your current battleboard signed off by a staff member, and pick up a fresh one. The time spent walking to Logistics and handling the swap counts toward the 10-minute rest period.

During a Rest, your character is recuperating by stretching, meditating, catching their breath, sharpening a blade, or whatever fits their personality. You may not use game skills, engage in combat, or take any other strenuous in-game action while resting. If any of these things interrupt your rest, you must start over.

During an event, a character may Rest a limited number of times: **(2 × event days) − 1**. For a typical 2-day event, this means 3 Rests. For a 3-day event, 5 Rests. Spend them wisely.

> [!NOTE] NPC Shifts and Resting 
> A player may choose to volunteer for an NPC shift during their rest time, earning additional rewards while their character recuperates. See the NPC section for details.

### Per-Day Abilities and Effects

Some abilities, effects, and magic items do not recharge through Resting. Instead, they recharge once per day at **6:00 PM** (out of game). This happens automatically and does not count as a Rest.

Production skills, certain magic items, and any ability or effect labeled "Per Day" or "Daily" follow this schedule.
